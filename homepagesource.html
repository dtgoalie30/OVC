WELCOME TO OTTAWA, ILLINOIS

Ottawa is a vibrant tourist destination located 80 miles outside of downtown Chicago. It is often considered to be In the Middle of Everywhere. You are within a stones throw of many diverse activitiesfrom lush and natural outdoor areas to renowned cultural and historic attractions.

Surrounded by open spaces, rivers, and trails, Ottawa is a natural setting that allows for people to get outdoors and enjoy some relaxation. We serve as a hub to 4 state parks (Starved Rock, Matthiessen, Buffalo Rock, Illini State Park) and a nature preserve all within 20 minutes of our downtown.
